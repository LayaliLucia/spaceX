# 🚀 Disruptive Technologies Project – SpaceX

As part of our **Final Exam Project** on disruptive technologies, we chose **SpaceX** as our case study.  
SpaceX represents a groundbreaking force in aerospace, redefining how humanity approaches space exploration and transportation.

## ✨ Project Overview
In this project, we:
- Researched **SpaceX** as a disruptive technology
- Analyzed its impact on aerospace, innovation, and society
- Prepared a **presentation** to share our findings with our professor

## 🎯 Purpose
The goal of this project was to:
- Understand what makes a technology *disruptive*
- Explore SpaceX’s role in lowering costs and increasing accessibility to space
- Highlight the broader implications for science, industry, and humanity

## 📌 Notes
This project was an academic exercise in analyzing disruptive technologies.  
It reflects our perspective on how **SpaceX** is reshaping the future of space travel.
